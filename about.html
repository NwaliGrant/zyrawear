Who Are We ?

Zyrawear is a proudly African fashion brand that celebrates the richness of culture through bold and modern design. Inspired by traditional patterns, street vibes, and the vibrant spirit of the continent, Zyrawear creates clothing and accessories that speak confidence and identity. Every piece is a blend of creativity, heritage, and contemporary style. Whether it’s Ankara-inspired jackets, Afro-urban tees, or statement accessories, Zyrawear is made for those who want to stand out and stay rooted at the same time. It’s more than fashion. It’s a movement of pride, expression, and African excellence.

One of the fabrics we honour deeply is the Akwàete cloth. This is a famous woven fabric made in Akwete town in Abia State, Nigeria. It is traditionally woven by women and is used by both men and women for cultural events. Akwàete is very popular in Igbo traditional attire and stands as a powerful symbol of craftsmanship, heritage, and elegance. At Zyrawear, we bring the legacy of fabrics like Akwàete into the modern spotlight through fresh, creative expressions.
